# CiviQ

An open-source civic education platform that makes learning politics, governance, democracy, and public policy fun, engaging, and accessible through AI-powered quizzes, humor, and gamification.

## Mission

Most people find politics boring, complicated, or inaccessible.

CiviQ aims to change that.

We use interactive quizzes, AI-generated challenges, satire, leaderboards, and rewards to help citizens understand:

1. Democracy
2. Elections
3. Governance
4. Constitutions
5. Public Policy
6. Political History
7 Civic Responsibility

Our goal is to create the most engaging civic learning platform for Africa and eventually the world.
