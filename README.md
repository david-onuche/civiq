# CiviQ

Learn Politics. Think Critically. Have Fun.

CiviQ is an open-source civic education platform that transforms the way people learn about politics, governance, democracy, constitutions, elections, and public policy through AI-powered quizzes, humor, storytelling, and gamification.

Our mission is to make civic knowledge accessible, engaging, and rewarding for everyone—students, young adults, professionals, educators, and lifelong learners.

## Why CiviQ?

Politics affects every aspect of society, yet many people find it confusing, boring, intimidating, or difficult to access.

Traditional civic education often relies on textbooks, lectures, and outdated learning methods that fail to engage modern audiences.

CiviQ changes this by combining:

1. Artificial Intelligence
2. Interactive Learning
3. Humor and Satire
4. Gamification
5. Civic Education

to create an experience that is both educational and entertaining.

Whether you're preparing to vote for the first time, learning about your constitution, exploring public policy, or simply testing your political knowledge, CiviQ helps you learn through play.

## Vision

To become the world's most engaging civic learning platform, empowering millions of citizens with the knowledge needed to participate meaningfully in democratic societies.

## Mission

To improve civic literacy by making political education:

1. Accessible
2. Engaging
3. Inclusive
4. Entertaining
5. Data-driven

## Core Learning Areas

### Democracy

Learn how democratic systems work and why they matter.

### Elections

Understand voting systems, electoral processes, and political participation.

### Governance

Explore how governments function at local, regional, and national levels.

### Constitutions

Learn about rights, responsibilities, institutions, and the rule of law.

### Public Policy

Discover how policies are created, implemented, and evaluated.

### Political History

Explore important historical events, leaders, movements, and political transitions.

### Civic Responsibility

Develop the knowledge and skills needed to become an active and informed citizen.

### International Politics

Understand diplomacy, global institutions, international relations, and world affairs.

# Features

## AI-Powered Quiz Engine

Generate unlimited educational questions using artificial intelligence.

Features include:

1. Context-aware questions
2. Country-specific content
3. Current affairs support
4. Multiple difficulty levels
5. Personalized learning paths

## Humor & Satire Mode

Learn through funny, sarcastic, and thought-provoking political scenarios.

Example:

> A politician promises to solve unemployment, inflation, traffic, and bad Wi-Fi in one week. What should citizens ask first?

A. How?
B. Why?
C. Show the plan.
D. All of the above.

## Daily Challenges

Complete daily civic missions and learning quests to earn rewards.
